7. Consolidation & Investment Recommendations

Key Finding: Extreme SQO Concentration

Just 4 of 13 campaigns (31%) produce 89% of all SQOs. ServiceTrade Brand alone drives 67% of pipeline opportunities (42 of 63). Meanwhile, 7 campaigns (54%) have produced zero SQOs — representing spend that doesn't convert to pipeline. This concentration creates both risk (Brand dependency) and opportunity (reallocation headroom).

SCALE — High Performers

  • ServiceTrade Brand — 42 SQOs, 21.5% MQL→SQO. Best full-funnel campaign. Increase branded search budget to capture more high-intent demand.
  • Fire Protection — 6 SQOs, 15.4% MQL→SQO, 51.3% MQL→SQL. Strong conversion at every stage. Scale search budget.
  • Competitive Search — 4 SQOs, 14.8% MQL→SQO. Growing MQL trend (1→6→5/month). Invest in expansion keywords.
  • Commercial HVAC — 4 SQOs, 9.8% MQL→SQO, 36.4% SQL→SQO rate. Solid mid-funnel performance. Scale where possible.

OPTIMIZE — Fix or Pivot

  • Fire Demo Request — 143 MQLs but only 4 SQOs (2.8% throughput). Huge volume with leaky funnel. Add qualification questions or tighten targeting.
  • Mechanical Demo Request — 175 MQLs, 3 SQOs (1.7% throughput). Worst efficiency among major campaigns. Qualification overhaul needed before more spend.
  • Retargeting — Small sample (6 MQLs, 3 SQLs) but 50% MQL→SQL shows promise. Needs scale to validate.

CONSOLIDATE / CUT

  • User Conference GDN — 13 MQLs, 0 SQLs, 0 SQOs. One-time event campaign with zero pipeline impact. Discontinue.
  • DSA Test — 9 MQLs, 0 SQLs, 0 SQOs, 0% Tier A+B. Pure Tier C leads with zero conversion. Cut or dramatically restructure.
  • Core Services — 3 MQLs, 0 SQLs. Insufficient volume and no pipeline signal. Consolidate into Brand or Competitive Search.
  • Demand Gen / Awareness — 2 MQLs each, 0 SQOs. Negligible impact. Reallocate budget to proven campaigns.

Consolidation Impact Estimate

Cutting the 5 zero-performing campaigns and reallocating their budget to Brand, Fire Protection, Competitive Search, and Commercial HVAC could yield an estimated 8–12 additional SQOs per quarter based on those campaigns' historical conversion rates. The Meta Demo Request campaigns (318 combined MQLs) need qualification gates — even a modest improvement from 2.2% to 5% MQL→SQO throughput would add ~7 SQOs from existing spend.
